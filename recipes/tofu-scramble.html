Tofu Scramble

Description

A simple tofu-scramble recipe that can be prepared in about 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 16oz Tofu
  • 1/2 medium Onion
  • 3/4tsp black salt
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2tsp onion powder
  • 1/4tsp tumeric powder
  • 3tbsp nutritional yeast

Steps

  1. Begin cooking the tofu in a medium skillet. Cook till most of the moisture has evaporated and the tofu has begun to brown.
  2. Add the onion and continue to cook till the onions begin to sweat.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and cook till lightly brown.
